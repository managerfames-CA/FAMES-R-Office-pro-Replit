## Intentionally outside Version 1

Login, Supabase/database server, cloud document storage, multi-device sync, client portal, notifications, digital signature and actual document/receipt binary upload remain out of scope.

## Consistency observations

The final application continues to use repository interfaces and namespaced localStorage. Dashboard and report figures derive from the same repositories. Locked/Closed records remain visible historically but are unavailable for new operational entries. No disconnected Phase 7 business dataset or new major module was introduced.
